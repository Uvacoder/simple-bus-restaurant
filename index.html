About Us

Seafood lovers, rejoice! All seafood, all of the time.

Pearl Grille is a family-owned and operated restaurant. Kevin Pearl and his wife, Maggie, have lived in North Carolina for most of their lives. They share a love for seafood, and a passion for serving their community.

Pearl Grille's doors opened in 2006, in Kure Beach, North Carolina.

Catering to the greater Wilmington area, Pearl Grille is equipped with a full bar, spacious dining area, and plenty of outdoor seating.

Pandemic protocols are in place for the safety of all parties; please call us if you have questions or concerns.
